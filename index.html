Skip the Wait, Order Ahead

Brisket

Brisket is a cut of meat from the breast or lower chest of beef or veal. The beef brisket is one of the nine beef primal cuts, though the definition of the cut differs internationally.

Smoked Chicken

Barbecue chicken consists of half a chicken smoked for hours to lock in the juices. The chicken is seasoned with a secret spice rub and topped with barbecue sauce.

Pork Ribs

Our pork ribs are locally sorced from farmers here in Indiana. we prepare them with a dryrub and they are smoked for hours until they fall off the bone. These are our best sellers!

Pulled Pork

Pulled Pork is shredded pork shoulder. It is slow-smoked over wood for yp to 11 hours!The meat is then shredded manually and mixed with a sauce. It may be served on bread or eaten on its own.
